Hi there!

I'm Maya De Marco

I am a Montreal based graphic and web designer who is always looking to learn, collaborate, and share with passionate individuals just like me. I aspire to help those I work with bring the love for their ideas to life.

I mostly enjoy working with branding, layout, illustration, and typography, however, I am capable of adapting to your needs to make your most creative and innovative ideas blossom. My design style is mostly clean, effective, organized, and serious but I will never pass up the opportunity to add a pop of colour and playfulness to my work. I strongly believe that conceptualization, intention, and meaning are at the heart of good design.
